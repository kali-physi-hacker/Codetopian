What we are

The Codetopia community is a software developing /Techno Training Ghana based organization/community consisting of Computer Science and I.T personnel from various Tech institutions. It was founded on the 25th of March in the year 2020. The community is mainly for both software and website developers to enhance training of young and upcoming computer scientists. The community is a sub-division of the its mother company MARATHIN-SOFT INC. (A company in due process of establishment). The community and company respectively was founded by Seth Mensah Amekudzi Lucky who is also the CEO and co-founded by Raphael Appiah-Kubi who is the Head of communications and global affairs of the community and company. And others whose name will later be disclosed. We are "Computer Scientists of the next generation".

What we do

Computer Science being an involving field of discipline, requires organisation and continues studiness, is not only a discipline involving writing of codes or programming, but mainly, solving of problems using computer programs. It may be thought of as a discipline whose primary task is to solve problems and that's we do. We design, create and write quality software whose primary goal is to solve a problem at hand. Therefore, we code not only for fun but also to solve problems. We "Solve Problems".

Our Vision

It is the sole vision of the codetopia community to create a utopia for Computer Scientists and Technology enthusiast to make an impact in the world in which we live in through science and technology Technology enthusiast and programmers/coders to assemble and CODE FOR CHANGE in this technological age.

Our Mission

Helping young and upcoming developers and other I.T field personnel build interest and courage in facing tech challenges in their technological fields. Training and specializing young developers and also other I.T personnel in their field of interest. Equip young programmers/coders and I.T personnel the necessary skills and tools for job opportunities on the techno market. VISION OF THE COMMUNITY

Our Goal

Making an impact in this world we live in through science and technology is our prime motive and passion. Imagination and Creativity lies within the edges of our brains. Limitation to it depends on your exposure. We are
